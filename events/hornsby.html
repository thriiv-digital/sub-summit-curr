About Suburban Business Summit — Hornsby

Join us at the Suburban Business Summit — Hornsby and connect with local business owners and leaders from trade, charities, finance, food manufacturing, council, auto trades, schools, health practitioners, and venue managers who understand the challenges you face.

You'll gain practical ideas and strategies you can use right away to help your business grow and succeed.

Meet 100+ like-minded professionals and industry experts, and build meaningful connections that open doors to new opportunities.

Learn from 28+ experienced speakers who share real-world insights tailored to our community's unique business landscape.

Thank You

Julian Leeser, Liberal Federal Member for Berowra, shared a community-focused, heartfelt message of support for the Suburban Business Summit — thanking organiser Luke Griffin, and reinforcing a commitment to help small businesses in Hornsby cut red tape, access more local business spaces, and create a thriving live-and-work community.
